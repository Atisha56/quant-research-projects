# Quantative Finance Research

Independent and co-authored quantitative finance research covering machine learning crash
prediction, event studies, panel econometrics, and time-series volatility modeling.

---

## Featured Projects

### [Network-Based Early Warning System for Equity Market Crashes](./Network-Crash-Early-Warning)
Composite Fragility Score from network biomarkers across 10 S&P 500 sectors, lifting crash-prediction AUC from 0.645 (VIX benchmark) to 0.726 using XGBoost. *(Co-authored, under journal review)*

### [Bank Failure Contagion — Event Study (Fama-French Three Factor)](./Bank-Failure-Contagion)
Event study on 37 U.S. bank failures (2015–2025), finding significant negative abnormal returns (t=-3.00***) in geographically proximate banks. 

### [Firm Investment & Volatility Modeling — U.S. Energy Sector](./Energy-Sector-Capex-Volatility)
Panel fixed-effects regression on capex drivers (247 firms, R²=94%) plus ARFIMA-GARCH/APARCH volatility modeling on ExxonMobil returns. 

### [Cryptocurrency Market Interdependence — VAR & DCC-GARCH](./Cryptocurrency-Market-Interdependence)
VAR(1) and DCC-GARCH(1,1) modeling across 5 cryptocurrencies, finding BNB offers the strongest diversification benefit (0.29 correlation vs. 0.74–0.75 among BTC/ETH/DOGE). 

### [GARCH Monte Carlo Risk Simulation (C++)](./GARCH-Monte-Carlo-Risk-Simulation)
GARCH(1,1) Monte Carlo simulation implemented from scratch in C++ (1M paths), estimating 1% VaR (7.88%) and Expected Shortfall (9.70%).
